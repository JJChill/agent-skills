# Brief: parcel locker network

We operate a network of self-service parcel lockers. Couriers deposit
parcels into locker compartments; recipients get a pickup code and
collect the parcel within a holding window. If the window lapses, the
parcel is returned to the depot. Retail partners host the lockers and
are paid per successful pickup.

Known from operations:

- A courier scans a parcel at the locker before a compartment opens.
- Recipients are notified when their parcel is deposited.
- About 4% of parcels lapse and get swept back to the depot weekly.
- Partners dispute payments when pickups are miscounted.
