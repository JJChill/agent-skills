# Story: Free delivery on Dave's books

As a book buyer, I want delivery to be free when my order contains a book
authored by Dave Farley, so that I'm encouraged to buy his books.

## Acceptance criteria

1. When a buyer with a domestic home address adds "Modern Software
   Engineering" by Dave Farley to their order, delivery is free.
2. When a buyer adds "Specification by Example" by Gojko Adzic to their
   order, delivery is NOT free.
3. When a buyer with a home address in New Zealand (international) adds
   "Modern Software Engineering" by Dave Farley to their order, delivery
   is NOT free.
4. When a buyer adds "Continuous Delivery" by Dave Farley and Jez Humble
   (co-authored) to their order, delivery is free.
