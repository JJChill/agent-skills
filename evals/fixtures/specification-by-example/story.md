# Story: waive pickup fee for loyalty members

As a loyalty member, I want the parcel pickup fee waived on my
collections, so that membership feels worthwhile.

What we know from the product owner so far:

- Standard pickups normally carry a €1 pickup fee.
- Loyalty members pay no pickup fee on standard pickups.
- The waiver applies only while the membership is active.

Not yet discussed: oversized parcels (which carry a €3 fee),
collections by a nominated neighbour, pickups during a lapsed
membership that is later renewed, and whether the waiver appears
as €0 or as a discount line.
