# Glossary

Terms are the ubiquitous language of this system. One term per concept.
Code, tests, specs, and conversation use these words exactly.

## Customer
A person or organization that holds a subscription and is billed for it.
NOT: "client", "user", "account holder".

## Subscription
A **Customer**'s ongoing entitlement to the service, billed each
**Billing Period**. States: active → past due → cancelled.
NOT: "plan", "membership".

## Billing Period
The interval a **Subscription** is billed for. Always one calendar month.
NOT: "cycle", "term".
